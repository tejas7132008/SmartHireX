# Welcome to SmartHireX

SmartHireX is an adaptive hiring interview platform that evaluates candidates with a rubric-aware, multi-agent process.

## What to Expect

- Upload a resume PDF to start.
- Wait up to 60 seconds while SmartHireX builds an initial candidate view.
- Continue the chat-based interview while the rubric panel updates over time.
- Review final reasoning with confidence and stakeholder decisions.
